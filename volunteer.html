Volunteer with Our Data Futures

If you’d like to be involved on a more regular basis, we are always looking for passionate and skilled volunteers to help. We need Researchers, UX/UI Designers, Cloud and Software Engineers, and Community Outreach and Fundraising specialists. Sign up if you’d like to join a very passionate group of Torontonians working to raise community awareness and engagement for Our Data Futures!!

Be interviewed

There are many ways to get involved. If you are interested in contributing your thoughts and ideas on Data Governance, book an interview with us. We’d love to hear from you. This is a 40 minute interview, where we gather what People of Toronto thinks of Data Governance. We need to speak with as diverse a group of Torontonians as people, so we need You!!
